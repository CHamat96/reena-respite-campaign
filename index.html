Help us give the gift of respite

Caring for someone with a developmental disability can be tiring - both physically and emotionally. The support of family, friends and community services is important.

Reena provides valuable respite programs to provide families, with a break from their care-giving responsibilities. Parents are able to spend time with other family members, see a movie, or take a much-needed vacation, knowing that their family member is in a caring and safe environment. Respite programs are tailored to the needs of the individual and include day as well as overnight options.

Your support acts as a vital "life-line" for caregivers, granting them the opportunity to take a break, rest, and rejuvenate themselves.

Give the Gift of Camp

Our camps focus on building social and life skills in a fun, supportive, and creative environment. Our seven-day Winter Break camp runs over the holidays and participants enjoy recreation and leisure-based activities including: arts and crafts, sports, cooking, cooperative games and music. Our five-day March Break camp offers community-based activities including: local excursions, sports, cooking, swimming and music.

Many campers require additional support, and the associated costs can be prohibitive for many families.

Donations play a crucial role in subsidizing camp costs and offering much-needed respite to families who require financial assistance
